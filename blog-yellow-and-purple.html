Yellow and Purple

Esra Ozgun · Apr 18, 2023 · 1 min read

It is not only a matter of learning the techniques but also a matter of time and patience. Colour mixing in botanical art is about understanding the subtle relationships between warm and cool tones — and how yellow and purple sit opposite one another on the colour wheel.

When you mix carefully, you can achieve luminous shadows and clean highlights without muddying the paper. Practice small swatches before committing to a full study; your eye will learn faster than your brush at first.
