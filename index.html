personal art teaching

Esra Ozgun is an Ottawa-based botanical artist and instructor teaching watercolour techniques for botanical illustration. She offers beginner and intermediate classes, including courses at the Ottawa School of Art. Explore the beauty of plants through mindful observation and hands-on painting in welcoming in-person workshops.
